Overview

Purpose

Welcome to Rapids Whitewater Rafting, your ultimate destination for thrilling whitewater adventures! Immerse yourself in the excitement of rushing rapids, surrounded by stunning landscapes. Our experienced guides ensure safety and unforgettable moments, catering to all levels of rafting enthusiasts. From beginners to seasoned adventurers, Rapids Whitewater Rafting offers a variety of trips in beautiful locations. Explore our site for trip details, safety protocols, and book your adrenaline-pumping experience today!

Audience

Our target audience for the water rafting website is adventure-seeking individuals between the ages of 18 and 40, who are passionate about outdoor activities and are looking for thrilling experiences. These individuals may have a moderate to high disposable income, allowing them to indulge in recreational pursuits. They are tech-savvy and commonly access websites through mobile devices for quick and convenient information.

Scenario Questions:

1. Who are the target customers?

Answer: Our target customers are young adults and middle-aged thrill-seekers who have a keen interest in outdoor adventures. They are likely to be professionals or students with an active lifestyle, seeking memorable experiences to break away from their routine.

2. What do they want?

Answer: Our target audience desires adrenaline-pumping water rafting experiences that provide a combination of excitement, challenge, and camaraderie. They seek a platform that not only offers information about different rafting locations but also helps them connect with like-minded individuals.

3. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?

Answer: The majority of our audience will access the website through mobile devices. They are likely to use smartphones for quick browsing and planning, as they are always on the go and prefer the convenience of mobile access.

By addressing these aspects, our water rafting website aims to cater to the specific needs and expectations of our target audience, providing them with a seamless and engaging online experience.
